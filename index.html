What is this?

Many companies use dark pattern techniques to make it difficult to find how to obtain your data. JustGetMyData aims to be a directory of urls to enable you to easily obtain your data from web services.

Got a site you think should be added? Make a Pull Request on GitHub.

Guide

The links above are colour-coded to indicate the difficulty level of obtaining the data:

  • easy - Simple process
  • medium - Some extra steps involved
  • hard - Cannot be fully obtained without contacting customer services
  • impossible - Cannot be obtained
